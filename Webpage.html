Why Japanese Railways Win

Wendover Productions is a channel that makes videos about interesting things around the world. In this video, he talks about how Japan's trains are super reliable and run on time, making it easy to get around, especially with lots of routes. Back in the day, the train system was losing money, so they split it up and sold it to companies who made things better.

Samarium SMP ep1: Farming Diamonds and Wheat

This video is a part of the Samarium SMP series made by James Graside my best friend, and this is his Minecraft channel where he displays his gameplay mechanics and tricks. The video shows him farming diamonds and wheat, which are two of the most valuable resources in the game. He uses a variety of tools and techniques to farm these resources, including using a diamond pickaxe to mine diamonds and using a wheat seed to plant wheat. The video is a great example of how to farm these resources in Minecraft for beginners.

Data Source: RobotAlbotross

But what is a neural network? | Deep learning chapter 1

This video is from 3Blue1Brown, a channel that's famous for making math super easy to understand with cool pictures. The video breaks down how neural networks work, using the example of teaching a computer to recognize handwritten numbers and since i've been learning about neural networks and building and training my own using PyTorch and TensorFLow for my AI project for my internship, this video has been a great help. It explains that these networks are built with layers of "neurons" (think of them as number holders) and connections with "weights" and "biases" that control how information travels through the network.
